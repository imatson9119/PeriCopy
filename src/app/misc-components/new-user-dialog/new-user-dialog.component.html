Welcome to Pericopy!

Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly
Colossians 3:16a

Welcome to Pericopy! The above passage from Colossians is the inspiration for this project- I believe in the surpassing worth and wisdom of God and his word above all else, and hope that this tool can help instill that belief in others.

Pericopy is a web application centered around the memorization of longer passages of scripture. To get started, simply create a goal and begin reciting the passage! To hear more about the tool, visit the info page.
